The Vision

Inspired by
Leonardo da Vinci.

Horacio Pagani arrived in Italy with a dream and a letter of recommendation from none other than Juan Manuel Fangio. He believed that art and science were one.

Born in Reggio Emilia in 1992, Pagani Automobili was forged from an obsession: that a hypercar must be as beautiful as a painting and as precise as a spacecraft. The Zonda — named after a warm South American wind — was that vision made manifest. Unveiled in Geneva, 1999, it shattered every convention.

Today, each Zonda is still built by fewer than 100 artisans. Still signed by human hands. Still unrepeatable.

— Horacio Pagani, Founder

"The difference between something good and something great is attention to detail."

Ownership

Your Questions,
Answered

Acquisition begins with a private consultation at the Pagani Atelier in San Cesario sul Panaro, Italy, or at one of our authorised global representatives. We do not sell through traditional dealerships. Fill in the Configure form and a Pagani specialist will contact you within 48 hours.

The Pagani Bespoke Programme is unlimited in scope. Choose your carbon-weave pattern, body color, interior leather, stitch colour, titanium hardware finish, and personalisation details such as engraved nameplates. Every element is hand-finished to your exact specification. Delivery includes a factory tour and personal handover by the Pagani team.

Each Zonda receives annual servicing at Pagani-certified workshops globally. A dedicated aftersales team provides direct contact with Pagani engineers. The Pagani Lifetime Care Programme ensures your vehicle's mechanical and cosmetic perfection is maintained in perpetuity — because a Zonda is not just a purchase, it is a relationship.

Factory tours are available exclusively to Pagani clients and prospective owners by private invitation. The experience includes a guided walk through all production stages, a personal meeting with artisans, and the opportunity to see your own vehicle during assembly. Contact us to arrange your exclusive visit.

Pagani Zondas have proven to be exceptional stores of value. Early C12 models have appreciated over 600% since new. The Cinque Roadster achieved $15M at auction. With only ~140 Zondas ever produced, scarcity and desirability are intrinsic. We advise all potential owners to consult with a specialist automotive investment advisor.
